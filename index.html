LEARN HOW TO DJ

BASIC SKILLS

A GOOD FOUNDATION IS THE BEST START!

Taking a DJ course is the fastest and best way to master the basics of DJ-ing. Therefore this is a smart choice. The DJ Basic Skills course is the first indispensable step in your DJ career.

During the DJ lessons you'll discover all the tips & tricks while being guided by professional DJs. Learn everything about beatmatching by sound, loops, effects and cue points.

Every high-end mixer and player from Pioneer DJ® is available for hands-on experience. In this course we teach the essential skills needed to become a successful DJ.

We guarantee that you will be able to DJ flawlessly after this course.

TOPICS OF THE COURSE:

  • Introduction to the latest Pioneer DJ equipment
  • Playing & Cue
  • Counting beats and bars
  • Beat-Matching basics
  • Understanding the structure of dance music
  • Cueing techniques & cue points
  • Basic EQ controls
  • Loops & Hot Cues
  • Basic Effects
  • Beat matching different tracks
  • Creating Hot Cues & memory points
  • And many more

CLASS DETAILS:

  • Duration: 1 months
  • Format: Live Class
  • Sessions per week: 6 Classes

BECOME A PROFESSIONAL DJ

ADVANCED SKILLS

BOOST YOUR DJ CAREER!

Follow the Advanced and Pro DJ Skills course to level up your techniques and performance abilities.

Students learn deeper mixing techniques, advanced transitions, crowd reading, harmonic mixing, live edits, and professional set building.

The course focuses heavily on practical training and real DJ performance preparation.

During the final exam, students perform a 30-minute live DJ mix to showcase their development and originality.

TOPICS OF THE COURSE:

  • Rekordbox & Traktor Introduction
  • Music downloading & organization
  • Understanding music formats
  • Hot Cues & Memory Cues
  • Mixing different genres
  • Harmonic Mixing
  • Advanced Rekordbox workflow
  • Mixer effects & loops
  • Build-up effects
  • Live edits
  • Crowd reading
  • DJ set planning
  • Demo mix recording
  • Basic recording/editing
  • MC knowledge
  • Social media marketing
  • Project overview & conclusion

  • Sessions per week: 6 Classes
